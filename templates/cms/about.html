Our Founding

Bearing Express was founded in August 2003 by Francois Boise as a general bearing supplier.

With diligent market research, we discovered a shortage in the market place for a specialist automotive bearing supplier. As we grew the business, we positioned ourselves as a specialist automotive Gearbox, Differential, and Wheel Bearing supplier. Since 2006 we have focused on building bearing kits, made up of bearings, seals, and bushes, to make it easy for our customers when ordering parts. Since then we have partnered with international companies, NSK bearings of Japan, SKF Bearings from Germany, and together with another company, we order OE manufactured gears from Germany. Bearing Express, to this end, is one of the very few registered distributors of NSK bearing products in South Africa.
